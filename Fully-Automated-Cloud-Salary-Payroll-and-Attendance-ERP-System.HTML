The Master Pipeline

From raw attendance bytes to a finalized PDF in the employee's inbox. A seamless 4-step automated journey.

1. Data Aggregation

System links monthly raw logs (Cols A-J) with the Master ID database, ensuring accurate profile mapping.

2. Financial Render

The V8 Engine applies fines, taxes, and bonuses, printing the final ledger starting from Column M.

3. Vault Archiving

Generates styled HTML-to-PDF blobs and organizes them in Google Drive by specific Year and Month folders.

4. Secure Delivery

Dispatches the PDFs as email attachments directly to the employee's corporate inbox in safe batches of 15.
